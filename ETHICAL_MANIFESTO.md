# Nexus CTO: Ethical Automation Manifesto

## The Problem
Everyday founders and freelancers are overwhelmed by the technical, financial, and operational complexity of running a modern digital business. They need CTO-level expertise but cannot afford enterprise salaries. We are solving this by building an **Autonomous CTO-as-a-Service**.

## Our Core Ethical Pillars

### 1. Human-in-the-Loop Financials (Circuit Breakers)
While our AI (`nexus-agent-framework`) integrates tightly with financial data (`sql-reconciliation-engine`), **no destructive or capital-moving action will ever execute without a 1-click human approval.** The AI acts as a high-speed analyst and drafter; the human acts as the final executive key.

### 2. Zero-Retention Data Privacy
Everyday folks cannot risk their proprietary financial or business logic being absorbed into public LLM training datasets. 
- All data parsed by the Nexus agent is routed through zero-retention API endpoints (e.g., enterprise Bedrock/Claude layers).
- Local infrastructure is strictly enforced using `aws-infrastructure-playbook`.

### 3. Open Foundations, Managed Execution
We believe in open-source empowerment. The core frameworks powering Nexus CTO are built in public and fully open-source. We monetize purely on **managed execution**—if you want the AI to run on our highly secure, optimized cloud environment 24/7 without you managing servers, you pay the $29/mo premium. If you want to self-host, you are free to do so.

### 4. Transparent Anti-Cheat Security
Our `anti-cheat-sdk` technology is used strictly to validate enterprise data integrity and client-side trust. It will never be deployed as spyware or hidden telemetry. The user is always in control of what the agent sees.
